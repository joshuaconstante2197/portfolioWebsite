Piano............... App

Html, Css and Javascript project.

This app allows the user to play real piano notes with a keyboard.

I used simple DOM maniputaltion and loops to map the keyboard to specific piano keys.

Normally, every sound will be repeated in a loop when a key is kept pressed but, I implemented a third party library, Howler.JS, to make sure that the notes are sustained as in a real piano.

The user can also control the volume of the notes being played.

I will also be adding functionalitty to play and practice simple piano songs.
